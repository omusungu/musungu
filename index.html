Verses' Message: "The Beloved Turns"

Based on Revelation 1:18, and devotion that God has for us. It is time to turn away from guilt and give God our hearts.

“Parts of Me Have Died” - Many times in life, we echo the words of the famous: "Parts of me have died." Guilt, failure, and change often bury pieces of us along the way. And truly, when a person dies, something dies with them—memories, dreams, influence, and presence.

Looking at the world’s religions, every religion has a founder who died - but only one has a founder who rose again and lives forever.

With their passing, something ended - be it their directions, guidance, presence, or power. Except one.

In Revelation 1:18, a unique voice speaks: “I am the Living One; I was dead, and now look, I am alive forever and ever! And I hold the keys of death and Hades.” This is not just survival—it is triumph over death itself.

Thus, the living founder, Jesus Christ, stands apart in three divine roles:

1. The Glory Revealer – “I am He who was dead and is now alive” His resurrection is not a secret—it is the revelation of God’s glory, a testimony that death could not hold Him.

2. The Eternal One – “I am alive forever and ever” Unlike others, His life is not subject to decay. He is the Alpha and Omega, transcending time.

3. The Throne Keeper – “I hold the keys of death and Hades” Authority rests in His hands. Not only did He pass through death—He now governs it.

If He holds the keys of death, what should we fear? Our pain, our loss, even the parts of us that feel like they've died - He can raise up.
